# MATILDA 🧠⚖️

MATILDA is an AI-powered legal and risk analysis platform that helps users understand hidden risks inside agreements, policies, and legal documents before accepting them.

Built for hackathons and rapid legal-tech innovation, MATILDA transforms complex legal language into human-readable insights, consequence simulations, and visual risk dashboards.

---

# 🚀 Features

## 📄 AI Document Analysis
- Upload PDF or DOCX files
- Paste raw legal text
- Extract important clauses automatically
- Detect risky language and obligations

---

## 🧠 Human Translation
Converts complex legal terminology into simple, understandable language.

Example:
> "The company may share your information with third parties"

instead of unreadable legal jargon.

---

## 💥 Consequence Simulator
MATILDA predicts real-world implications of accepting an agreement:

- Privacy exposure
- Data tracking risks
- Financial liabilities
- Legal consequences
- Third-party data sharing

This is the core differentiator of the platform.

---

## 📊 Risk Dashboard
Visual overview of:
- Overall risk score
- Legal risk
- Financial risk
- Privacy risk
- Data-sharing exposure
- Final AI recommendation

---

## 📁 Export Full Report
Generate downloadable AI-generated legal risk reports.
